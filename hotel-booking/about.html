Our Mission

Making Pakistan's
Finest Hotels
Discoverable

Pakistan is home to some of the world's most extraordinary hotels — from 5-star landmarks on Lahore's Mall Road to intimate heritage inns nestled beside Karakorum glaciers. Yet for too long, finding them required insider knowledge.

PakStay was founded to change that. We believe every traveller in Pakistan — whether visiting from abroad or exploring their own country — deserves access to the finest stays, presented honestly and beautifully.

We work directly with hotel owners, visiting every property in person before listing it, to ensure every hotel you see on PakStay genuinely meets the standard it claims.

Our Principles

What We Stand For

Radical Transparency

We show every hotel exactly as it is — no stock photos, no misleading descriptions. What you see on PakStay is what you get when you arrive.

Partnership with Owners

We see hotel owners as partners, not just suppliers. We invest time in understanding their properties and helping them present their best selves.

Uncompromising Standards

Every property is personally visited before listing. If it doesn't meet our standards, it doesn't appear — no matter how much they pay to list.

Passion for Pakistan

We're Pakistani and we're proud of it. Our deepest motivation is showing the world — and Pakistanis themselves — how extraordinary this country truly is.

Guest-First Always

Every decision we make is filtered through one question: is this better for our guests? Support, policy, and design all start with the traveller.

Sustainable Tourism

We actively promote eco-conscious properties and responsible tourism practices, particularly in Pakistan's fragile mountain ecosystems.
